Questions creators ask
How do you access my videos?

Through YouTube's official Data API, with your explicit consent — you sign in with Google and grant caption access for your own channel. Nothing is scraped, and you can revoke access at any time from your Google account.

What exactly do I get?

For each video: a ready-to-paste block of timestamps + chapter titles, formatted for the description (starting at 0:00, the format YouTube requires). It's plain, editable text. Edit any title, paste it when you like — it's yours forever, even if you never use YouChop again.

Will the titles just say “Introduction”?

No — that's YouTube's auto-chapters. YouChop writes topic titles from the transcript: what's actually discussed, phrased the way people search. You can edit anything before pasting.
