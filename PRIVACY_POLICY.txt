CHAPTR PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: November 17, 2024

DATA COLLECTION
Chaptr does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

LOCAL STORAGE ONLY
The extension uses Chrome's local storage API to save:
- Demo credit balance (stored locally on your device)
- Extension settings and preferences

All data remains on your device and is never sent to external servers.

DEMO MODE
This version operates in demo mode with:
- No backend server connections
- No analytics or tracking
- No user accounts or authentication
- All processing happens locally in your browser

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED
The extension requests these permissions:

- storage: Save settings locally in your browser
- tabs: Open welcome page when extension is installed
- identity: Reserved for future authentication (not used in demo)
- youtube.com: Access YouTube pages to inject the Chapterize button

NO THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
Demo mode does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

FUTURE VERSIONS
When the full version launches with backend features, this policy will be updated to reflect:
- User authentication
- Credit purchase processing
- Chapter caching for faster performance

Users will be notified of any changes to this privacy policy.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Website: https://chaptr.app

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

By using Chaptr, you agree to this privacy policy.
